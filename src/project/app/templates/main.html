Join the Green Revolution

Earn points for sustainable actions and make your campus greener

Dashboard

The Green Point System is a campus-wide initiative designed to inspire students to actively participate in sustainability and social awareness activities. Every eco-friendly action—such as waste collection drives, awareness campaigns, and community service—earns you Green Points. At the end of each semester, students with the highest points are rewarded by the college with exciting incentives including cash prizes, free online certification courses, and other exclusive perks. By taking part, you not only gain valuable skills and recognition but also contribute to a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable campus. Together, we’re building habits that protect our environment and promote responsible living for a better future.
